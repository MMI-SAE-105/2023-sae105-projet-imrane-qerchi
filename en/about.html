About us

This website was created by myself, Imrane QERCHI. Although it was made as part of an educational project, the film Princess Mononoke is particularly dear to my heart. I first watched it during my childhood, and it left a lasting impression on me. I rediscovered it du-ring high school, and since then, I regularly rewatch it. The themes it explores resonate with me, and the film has helped shape the person I am today. More than a school project, this site is a declaration of love to Princess Mononoke and its universe.
